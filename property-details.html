Enjoy exquisite bay, golf course, and ocean views from this luxurious 4 bedroom 4.5 bathroom, 5,192sf Penthouse located in Ocean Club Residences in the exclusive gated beachfront and golf course community of Ocean Club Estates on Paradise Island. This Penthouse has an open floorplan with living room, dining room, and TV room all leading out to an expansive covered terrace overlooking the pools, marina and harbour. The spacious master suite features hardwood floors, an office, and his & her bathrooms. The master bedroom has serene harbour views while her master bathroom overlooks the golf course and ocean beyond. All of the bedrooms have ensuite bathrooms and also private covered balconies with golf course and sea views.
